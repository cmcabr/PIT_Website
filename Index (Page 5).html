A Day in the Life

  1. Wake up around 5:00 or 6:00 and make coffee.
  2. Get dressed, brush teeth, and possibly do some more homework.
  3. Do some chores and leave.
  4. Wait for the bus and do more homework on the bus.
  5. Do some more homework or go to the library before class.
  6. Classes start.
    1. World Geography - Learn about the history of countries, climate, and religion (At Least So Far).
    2. Theatre and Media Communications - Learn the history of, styles of, and how to create, movies.
    3. Biology I - Learn about biological factors, cycles, and relationships.
    4. English I - Self-explanitory.
    5. Geometry I - Learn about how to solve for angles, rays, graphs, et cetera.
    6. Principles of Information Technology - All about computers and computer science.
    7. Football - Being an assistant for the football coaches,as well as filming the Varsity football games.
  7. Come home and do more chores.
  8. Take a shower, then eat dinner.
  9. Do even more homework.
  10. Sleep.
